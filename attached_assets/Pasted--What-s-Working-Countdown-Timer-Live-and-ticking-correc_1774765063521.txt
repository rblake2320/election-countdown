🐛 Bugs Found
Bug 1 — Dot nav indicator is inverted
The two dot indicators are backwards relative to their content. The left dot (index 0) activates when 2028 Presidential is shown, and the right dot activates when 2026 Midterm is shown. It should be chronological left-to-right (2026 left, 2028 right).
Bug 2 — No way to restore hidden quotes
After clicking "Hide Quote" (the × button below the quote), the entire quote section disappears with no button to bring it back. The user has to refresh the page. A "Show Quote" toggle or persistent button should be added. The "Hide Quotes" option inside the Settings dropdown has the same problem.
Bug 3 — Countdown target is off by one day
The 2026 Midterm countdown targets November 2 at midnight Eastern, but Election Day 2026 is November 3 (first Tuesday after Nov 1). The countdown should target Nov 3 midnight ET.
Bug 4 — Collapse state persists through hot reload
After collapsing the countdown with the ^ button and doing an F5 reload, the page reloads in the collapsed state rather than always opening expanded. There's no localStorage value causing this — it appears to be a React state or HMR artifact. In a cold-load production environment this may not occur, but it's worth verifying.
Minor — Header logo briefly disappears after dark/light toggle (transient visual glitch, corrects itself).
